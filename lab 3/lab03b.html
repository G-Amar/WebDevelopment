Grumpy Cat

Her name is Tardar Sauce (named after tartar sauce). She has feline dwarfism and an underbite, which makes her look like she is always grumpy. She has become a media sensation. Her Facebook page has over 8 million Likes.

Dwarf Cats

Dwarf cats are those that have dwarfism due to a genetic mutation. Their legs are abnormally short.

Dwarf cats have been selectively breeded to be sold to humans as pets, however, the ethics of their selective breeding is highly controversial, and many countries deem it cruelty to animals and prohibit the practice.

Cats!

The cat (scientific name: Felis catus) is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae and is commonly referred to as the domestic cat or house cat.

Felidae also contains many other species such as Tigers, Cougars, Servals, etc.
